Privacy Policy

UFood is a private personal recipe app. It is designed to help users create, organize, photograph, favorite, and revisit their own recipes locally on device.

No account. No ads. No tracking. Your recipes stay on your device.

Data Collection

UFood does not collect, sell, or track personal data. The app does not require a login account and does not include third-party advertising or tracking.

  • No user accounts
  • No third-party tracking
  • No ads
  • No social recipe feed
  • No public recipe marketplace

Recipe Data

Recipes, favorites, open counts, app rating, language choice, theme choice, and haptic preference are stored locally on your device.

Photos

UFood may use Camera or Photo Library access only when you choose to add a photo to a recipe. Recipe images are stored locally for use inside the app.

Nutrition Estimates

UFood may estimate calories, protein, carbs, fats, fiber, and recipe categories based on the ingredients you enter. These values are approximate only and are not intended as medical, dietary, or professional nutrition advice.

Internet Access

UFood does not require an internet connection for its core recipe features. There is currently no backend account system, online recipe feed, or cloud database.

Children’s Privacy

UFood does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

If this privacy policy changes, updates will be reflected on this page.
